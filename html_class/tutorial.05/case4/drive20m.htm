SureRite Drive 20M

The SureRite 20M 3.5-inch drive meets the storage needs of low-cost entry level and home desktop PCs. The 20M satistifes the requirements of the small footprint and low power standards of today's desktop systems, and features the AT-Z interface. The 20M targets the consumer for whom capacity, performance and price are equally important.

Features

  • 100 gigabytes
  • 15.2 Mbytes per second data-transfer rate
  • 12 milisecond seek time
  • Quiet operation
  • Easy installation
  • Three year limited warranty
