SureRite Drive 33M

The SureRite 33M 3.5-inch drive is perfect for the needs of small businesses and home users who are looking for more from their disk drives. The 33M features a small footprint and has low power requirements needed for today's multimedia systems. The system has the AT-ZM+ multimedia interface for lightning fast action. The 33M targets the small business owner who is willing to pay a little more for superior performance.

Features

  • 120 gigabytes
  • 10.0 Mbytes per second data-transfer rate
  • 9.9 milisecond seek time
  • Multimedia-Ready for full-screen, full-motion video playback and MMX
  • Quiet operation
  • Easy installation
  • Three year limited warranty
