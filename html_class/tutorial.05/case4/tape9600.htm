SureSave Tape Drive 9600

Warner's high-capacity DAT drive, the SureSave 9600, offers low cost multigigabyte data protection for workstations and servers. Choose from 16, 32 or, for maximum protection, 96 Gbytes DAT drives for your server or network. Add the Warner NetSave interface for increased speed and reliability. As with our other tape drives, the 9600 is backed by our industry-leading five year limited warranty.
