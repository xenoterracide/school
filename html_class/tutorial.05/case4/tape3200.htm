SureSave Tape Drive 3200

The external SureSave 3200 works with virtually any personal computer, portable or notebook. The 3200 automatically recognizes Enhanced Parallel Ports (EPP) and IEEE 1284 ports for maximum performance. Just plug the 3200 into the printer port of your computer and turn it on for quick and easy backup. The parallel port doesn't slow down the 30MB per minute speed of the 3200, making it the perfect portable solution. The 3200 is rugged, built for travel and backed by Warner's three year limited warranty. On the go? Go with the 3200!
